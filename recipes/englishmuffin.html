"Classic English Muffin Sandwich"

Description

There may be no better bread vehicle for a breakfast sandwich than an English
muffin. With its nooks and crannies that toast up just right and with a
texture that stands up to ingredients but is not too tough or cumbersome to
manage, it's pretty much the ideal. So why not start with this classic?

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Egg
  • English Muffin
  • Oil
  • Arugula
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Toast the English Muffin(s) in the toaster or dry Skillet.
  2. Fry two pieces of bacon on skillet.
  3. Cut bacon into quarters to fit in the sandwhich.
  4. Oil pan and fry the egg(s)
  5. Using a spoon baste the egg whites as required
  6. Toss a handful of arugula in a light coat of hot sauce
  7. Slide the fried egg(s), bacon, and arugula onto the bottom half of the muffin and top with the other half of the muffin
